# 📱 EliMorse User Manual

## 1. Description
**EliMorse** is an interactive educational application designed to teach you the international Morse code. Whether you want to translate messages, practice through games, or send signals using your phone's hardware, EliMorse provides all the tools in one place.

## 2. Introduction
The app helps users bridge the gap between plain text and the rhythmic world of dots and dashes. 
*   **What it does:** Translates text, plays audio/light signals, provides practice exercises, and tracks your progress.
*   **Who it is for:** Students, hobbyists, or anyone interested in communication history and emergency signaling.

## 3. Requirements
*   **Android Version:** Android 7.0 (Nougat) or higher.
*   **Permissions:**
    *   **Camera & Flashlight:** Required to send Morse signals via light.
    *   **Audio:** Required for voice guidance and Morse sounds.
    *   **Internet:** Used for basic network features.
    *   **Notifications:** Required for the Reminder feature.

## 4. Installation and Launch
1.  Install the app via the provided APK file or through your development environment.
2.  Upon launch, you will see a **Welcome Screen** with an animation.
3.  Wait for the timer to finish or press **Start** to proceed to the login/registration screen.

## 5. Interface Overview
*   **Welcome Screen:** The entry point with the app logo.
*   **Login/Registration Screen:** Where you create your account to save your scores.
*   **Dashboard (Main Menu):** The central hub to access all features.
*   **Translation Screen:** A split-screen for converting text and Morse.
*   **Exercise Screen:** Choose between Numbers, Letters, Words, or Listening modes.
*   **History Screen:** A list of your past exercise results.
*   **Reminder Screen:** A place to schedule your next study session.

## 6. Main Functions

### 🔠 Translation
*   **Location:** Dashboard -> "Translate" button.
*   **Steps:**
    1.  Enter your text in the top field.
    2.  Press the **Translate** button.
    3.  See the result in the bottom field.
    4.  *(Optional)* Press the **Switch** button to change from "Text to Morse" to "Morse to Text".
*   **Result:** Instant conversion. Unknown characters appear as `?`.

### 🔦 Sending Signals (Flashlight & Audio)
*   **Location:** Translation Screen.
*   **Steps:**
    1.  Translate a message or type Morse code manually.
    2.  Press the **Audio** button to hear the dots and dashes.
    3.  Press the **Flashlight** button to see your phone's LED blink the code.
*   **Result:** Real-world signals sent directly from your device.

### 🎮 Practice Exercises
*   **Location:** Dashboard -> "Exercise".
*   **Steps:**
    1.  Select a category (e.g., Numbers).
    2.  Press **Next**.
    3.  Look at the word/number on the screen.
    4.  Tap the **Dot**, **Dash**, and **Space** buttons to enter the code.
    5.  Press **Ready** to check your answer.
*   **Result:** Correct answers move you forward; mistakes are counted and saved.

### ⏰ Setting Reminders
*   **Location:** Dashboard -> "Reminder".
*   **Steps:**
    1.  Pick a date and time using the pop-up windows.
    2.  Press **Set Alarm**.
    3.  Alternatively, enter a number of seconds and press **Add Time Alarm**.
*   **Result:** Your phone will notify you when it is time to practice.

## 7. Usage Scenarios
*   **Scenario A (Learning):** A user wants to learn how to sign "SOS". They go to the **Translate** screen, type "SOS", and then press **Audio** and **Flashlight** multiple times to memorize the rhythm.
*   **Scenario B (Testing):** A user goes to **Exercises**, selects "Numbers", and completes 10 rounds to see how few mistakes they can make. They then check the **History** to compare with yesterday's score.

## 8. Troubleshooting
| Problem | Cause | Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Flashlight doesn't blink | Permission denied or Camera in use. | Ensure you allowed Camera permissions and close other camera apps. |
| No sound heard | Volume is low or "Silent" mode is on. | Check your media volume settings. |
| Morse translation shows `?` | You entered a symbol not supported by Morse. | Use only letters A-Z and numbers 0-9. |

## 9. Pro Tips
*   **Voice Assistant:** The app tells you which section you are entering. Listen carefully to navigate without looking!
*   **Theme Switcher:** Click the "Eli-Morse" logo on the Dashboard to change between Light and Dark modes.
